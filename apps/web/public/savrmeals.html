FAQ
Common questions
How far in advance do I need to order?▾
Orders must be placed at least 24 hours in advance. The daily cutoff is 2:00 PM — orders placed before 2 PM unlock the next-day pre-order discount automatically.
Can I change or cancel my pre-order?▾
Yes! You can modify or cancel your order up to 4 hours before your scheduled pickup or delivery time, free of charge.
How does the prepaid wallet work?▾
Add credits to your SavrWallet and use them for any order. Wallet credits earn an extra 5% bonus on every reload — it's the cheapest way to pay per meal.
Is there a minimum commitment?▾
No! You can order single meals with no subscription. Memberships are month-to-month with no lock-in. Cancel anytime from your dashboard.
What areas do you deliver to?▾
We currently serve the Tampa Bay area including Brandon, Tampa, St. Pete, and Clearwater. Pickup locations are available at multiple spots. Delivery is via third-party partners (DoorDash, UberEats).

Our story

We built SavrMeals because eating well shouldn't be expensive or complicated

It started with a simple question: why do people who plan ahead pay the same — or more — than people who decide last minute? That's backwards. Food should reward smart planning.

SavrMeals is a smart prepaid meal platform built around one core idea: by knowing what people want before we cook it, we eliminate waste, cut costs, and pass the savings directly to our customers.
